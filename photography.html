PHOTOGRAPHS

The art, science, and practice of creating images with light or other devices, whether chemically or electronically, is known as photography. The term is derived from the Greek words photos, which means light, and grophos, which means drawing.

The National Museum of the Philippines (NMP) houses the National Fine Arts Collection (NFAC) photographic collection, and three photographic exhibitions are currently on display in its regional museums.

Mount Pulag is the 0.33 maximum mountain withinside the Philippines and the very best top in Luzon. This photograph, taken in 2015 indicates the breathtaking surroundings of the mountain famous amongst hikers and nature lovers.

Photographer: Neal Oshima

This photo is a part of the exhibition, Pagkaing Filipino: Images of Regional Cuisine Dishes, Drinks, and Delicacies and their memories via way of means of Neal Oshima, presently exhibited on the Ilocos Regional Museum. Palapa is a famous highly spiced facet dish in Maranao cuisine. This condiment is made from sakurab, a kind of shallot, ginger, coconut, onions, garlic, chilli, and salt.
